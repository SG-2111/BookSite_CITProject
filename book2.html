Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

J K Rowling


  • First Released: 26 June 1997

  • Genre: Fantasy

  • Description: Harry Potter has never even heard of Hogwarts when the letters start dropping on the doormat at number four, Privet Drive. Addressed in green ink on yellowish parchment with a purple seal, they are swiftly confiscated by his grisly aunt and uncle. Then, on Harry's eleventh birthday, a great beetle-eyed giant of a man called Rubeus Hagrid bursts in with some astonishing news: Harry Potter is a wizard, and he has a place at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. An incredible adventure is about to begin!

  • Popular Rating: 4.47 / 5

  • About The Author:

    • J.K. Rowling is a British author best known for writing the Harry Potter series, a globally popular fantasy novel series that has been adapted into a successful film franchise. She also writes crime fiction under the pen name Robert Galbraith and has written companion volumes to the Harry Potter series. Rowling's full name is Joanne Rowling, and she adopted the pen name J.K. Rowling because she was advised to use initials to appeal to a broader audience, particularly young male readers.
